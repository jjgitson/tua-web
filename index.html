Our Origin

TUA Inc. began with a belief that meaningful technology should be accessible, usable, and beneficial to the people and environments where it is needed most.

Our foundation was built through years of research on how humans work and interact with systems. Working with organizations such as Samsung, Hyundai Motor, and Microsoft, our team studied user behavior and physiological responses during real-world tasks.

With expertise rooted in cognitive science and clinical research, we developed structured methods to translate measurable human experience data into practical insights.
