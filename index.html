Our Origin

TUA Inc. began with a belief that meaningful technology should be accessible, usable, and beneficial to the people and environments where it is needed most. Our foundation was built through years of research on how humans work, and interact with systems.

Working with organizations such as Samsung, Hyundai Motor, and Microsoft, our team studied user behavior, and physiological responses during real-world tasks. With expertise rooted in cognitive science and clinical research, we developed structured methods to translate measurable human experience data into practical insights.

This evidence-based mindset now guides our work distributing advanced solutions that support healthy aging, pain management, and physical function assessment across healthcare, community, and workplace settings.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted bridge connecting scientifically validated healthcare technologies with the markets, organizations, and communities that can benefit from them.

Our Mission

We support evidence-based solutions in physical function, mobility, pain reduction, and aging science to successfully enter and grow in new regions through education, localization, and sustained implementation.

Our Process

We intentionally work with a small number of high-impact products at a time. We build early clinical and operational confidence, generate proof-of-value, refine messaging, and establish pathways for stable adoption—ultimately empowering partners to operate independently and sustainably.

Our Team

We are a multidisciplinary group with backgrounds in clinical research, ergonomics, UX, and aging science. Our shared focus is translating complex but meaningful health and performance technologies into practical tools that improve daily function, reduce burden, and support better aging outcomes.
