Our Origin

TUA Inc. began with a belief that meaningful technology should be accessible, usable, and beneficial to the people and environments where it is needed most. Our foundation was built through years of research on how humans work and interact with systems.

Working with organizations such as Samsung, Hyundai Motor, and Microsoft, our team studied user behavior and physiological responses during real-world tasks. This evidence-based mindset now guides our work.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted bridge connecting scientifically validated healthcare technologies with the markets, organizations, and communities that can benefit from them.

Our Mission

We support evidence-based solutions in physical function, mobility, pain reduction, and aging science to successfully enter and grow in new regions through education, localization, and sustained implementation.
