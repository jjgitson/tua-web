Our Origin

TUA Inc. began with a belief that meaningful technology should be accessible, usable, and beneficial to the people and environments where it is needed most.

Collaborating with organizations like Samsung, Hyundai Motor, and Microsoft, we studied user behavior and physiological responses to translate measurable data into actionable clinical insights.

How We Work

A practical, evidence-oriented approach to localizing and scaling validated healthcare technologies in Korea.

  1. 01 Discovery

    Define the right market fit

  2. 02 Validation
